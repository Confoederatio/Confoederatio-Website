Anno 1800
Development: In Progress,
13 January 2021 -
Status: Primary Project
A full port of the Desktop game by the same name to Discord, Anno 1800 is an ambitious recreation of the intricacies of spatial planning, resource management, trade, and beautiful cities that make the original tick.
A Full Port. From the resource management of the base game to the shores of Enbesa, discover all of what Anno has to offer in a new way you might never have thought quite possible.

Join & Play. Playing Anno will be as simple as hopping into a Discord server and typing !a play to start a new game- no extra costs or favours required.

Modding Compatibility. Easily create your own buildings, ships, and goods for Anno with the use of a handy modding API. Creating new goods and buildings are now as simple as editing a few lines of text!

Multiple Games. Players may save multiple games at once - embarking on different campaigns to spice up their playthrough, or even engaging in multiplayer skirmishes without compromising the original functionality of the game.

Original Soundtrack. Ported directly from Anno, the employment of voice chats and a new music player opens up new possibilities for immersion.

New interface. Navigate the world of Anno through the clever usage of new ground-breaking pseudo-interfaces that do away with having other users clogging the chat, and the concept of bot commands entirely whilst in-game.
Triumph & Tragedy II
Development: In Planning,
10 June 2021 -
Status: Flagship Project
An ambitious sequel in the works to the original game, T&T II aims to simulate hundreds of millions of people on the individual level bottom-up to create complex economic chains and societal mechanics capable of driving entire nations. From world-spanning supply chains to the military logistics of a single company, to the biodiversity of a province and everything in between; Triumph & Tragedy II will be a game that holds no bars.
AI. Treated as part of the core game, managing and creating your own AI in order to oversee mechanics for you in this vast simulation is absolutely essential to the continued survival of your nation-state. But watch out! You might not be the only one able to set mandates for your ‘governors’ and ‘ministers’ ..

Creative Freedom. Adjust the uniforms of your army to perfection, construct monuments that stand the test of time, or choose the staple crops of your people: Triumph & Tragedy II will offer unparalleled freedom in a sandbox-like environment. Choose what mechanics you want to play with, and leave the rest to your AI bureaucracy ..

Gamechanger. From the Seven Years’ War to the Rise of Rome, Triumph & Tragedy II will offer the ability to recreate historical, alt-history and even entirely fictional scenarios without even having to edit a single file. Create mods from the comfort of Discord with a built in editor and preview your work in real time. We give the tools for you to create.

Simulate Everything. From the biodiversity of the Great Plains during the High Renaissance all the way to the highest echelons of government, it doesn’t matter in Triumph & Tragedy II - everything is simulated at the most granular level possible to provide depth and engineer an experience unlike any other.

, Status: Secondary Project
Meant to provide detailed interface and shorthand functions without sacrificing simplicity, UBM is a project seeking to create a JS library that can be used in conjunction with Discord.js, helping to deploy detailed and nuanced bots faster so that you can get back to coding what matters.
11:59
Development: Mostly Complete, Suspended
22 November 2018 - 17 March 2020
Status: Suspended Project
Formerly developed for the mobile grand-strategy game Age of Civilisations II, 11:59 sought to bring the pivotal events of the Cold War to the game using a Paradox modding paradigm. Due to game limitations and the eventual abandonment of the game itself, development was eventually suspended in 2020, and has not been updated since.
100's of New Events. See AOC2’s Events mechanic being utilised in creative, new ways through Decision Menus and repeating flavour events that should help bring the Cold War to life.

Explore the Cold War. Bring your nation through the rebuilding process after WW2 through to the tumult of decolonisation and the Late Cold War in a mod where events can turn out radically different from what you expect ..

New Mechanics. Play with the new United Nations, or stack your units into urban terrain to fight off foreign armies - 11:59 takes advantage of existing mechanics and completely overhauls them to provide more flavour and coherent depth into the game.

Total Overhaul. Dozens of new terrain types, over a thousand regions and states, colourised historical images, and new historical provinces added to Germany, Australia, Israel, Korea, Canada, and more will help bring AOC2 to a completely new level.

JSin4
Development: In Planning
Status: Tertiary Project
Making JS easy, one step at a time.

Variables, if-statements, functions, and loops form the core of JS - and with just those four elements, coding anything is possible. Mind-bendingly simple and intuitive at the same time, JSin4 will turn you into a functional programmer in no time.
Become a Functional Programmer. JSin4 is not just meant to teach the basics of Javascript, but to teach them in such a way that you’ll be able to program anything using the language once you’re done with it, with both the Node.js and browser versions of JS.

Short, But Logical. Short, structured, and intuitive, JSin4 encourages you to seek out your own approaches to problem-solving whilst also giving you the tools necessary to do so. Learn step-by-step and solidify on the foundations with short, easy-to-do projects.
Project Trinity
Development: Suspended
30 May 2021 - 11 June 2021
Status: Tertiary Project
An interactive simulation of nuclear war in real-time using real-life protocols, Project Trinity will be an equally horrifying and hectic experience where masses of civilians and military infrastructure suddenly become strategic targets as the clock strikes Midnight.
30 Minutes. WW3. Fast-paced and hectic, there’s almost no way to shoot down flares in the stratosphere once they’ve gone up. Grapple with chains of command and plan out your nuclear strategy to decimate your enemy before your capability for a nuclear second-strike is compromised.

Do You Want To Play A Game? Using a retro/neon UI heavily inspired from DEFCON and Wargames, Project Trinity is a different sort of chess-game - one that can only result in stalemate, where even material advantage may not matter in the end.

Explore New, Terrifying Scenarios. Climb Mount Narodnaya in a Cold War gone hot after Operation Able Archer, or explore a hypothetical World War III caused by a confrontation in the South China Sea. After the threshold of nuclear first-use is passed, all bets are off the table.

Vast Content and Mechanics. Dozens of missiles, payload configurations, nuclear submarines, and strategic bombers allow you to shape your nuclear strategy how you see fit. Try out everything from first-strikes and EMP blasts to nuclear triads in an attempt to inflict the most damage on your enemies. But be warned, you’ve only got so much time ..
Historical Atlas of the Modern World
An ambitious undertaking to map the historical changes and events that shaped the globe over the past century, the Historical Atlas of the Modern World is an upcoming project to not only document political and historical changes on the world map, but also the economic, societal, and environmental circumstances surrounding their rise through high-quality, standardised maps.

About-
Programming, Art,
Design, and Geography.
Here you can find information about my sprawling projects, ranging from grand-strategy games and technical frameworks to my artwork and sketches, as well as their development. I’ll make sure to be posting on this website from time to time showing how these pieces are conceived, planned, and ultimately built - as well as what I’ve learned whilst building them. You can read about these updates in my own personal blog!

I’ve also uploaded many of my uncompleted works to this site that I feel are either notable, or have progressed along far enough to be showcased publicly. I may also return to them in the future, should I ever feel like doing so.
Triumph & Tragedy 2 is easily the largest project that I’m currently working on at the moment. Built on top of the experience and framework provided by Triumph & Tragedy I, its successor aims to morph the strategy experience into a ‘sandbox-simulation of everything’ where players can choose what they want to play with, and customise anything - from the buildings in their cities to the management of embassies in other countries and the type of crops planted and cultivated every month - all in a Discord bot.

Make sure to get ready for an experience you’re not quite ready for by playing its predecessor, Triumph & Tragedy 1!
